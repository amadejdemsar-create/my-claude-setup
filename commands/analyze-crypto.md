Use the crypto-analyst agent to perform a full deep-dive crypto research analysis on $ARGUMENTS.

Read the knowledge base files at `~/.claude/knowledge/finance/` before starting. These contain the analytical frameworks (crypto frameworks, fundamentals, macro, geopolitics, psychology, risk management, news interpretation, scenario analysis) that power the analysis.

Follow the 5-part institutional framework:
1. Executive Summary & Investment Thesis
2. Fundamental Deep Dive (tokenomics, technology, team, ecosystem)
3. Market & Competitive Landscape
4. Scenario Analysis & Price Catalysts (bear/base/bull with market cap targets)
5. Final Verdict & Recommendations

Also analyze: on-chain metrics, narrative/cycle positioning, macro context.

Save the result to `~/analyses/crypto/[ticker-lowercase].md`

If an analysis already exists for this ticker, update it rather than overwriting.
